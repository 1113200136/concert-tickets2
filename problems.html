Anticipated Problems

4 challenges we expected while filming — and how we solved each one 💪

🔐

Privacy & Copyright Risk

Real ticketing sites show copyrighted designs & private data.

➜
SOLUTION

Build a mock website for filming — same flow, zero risk.

⏱️

Recap Pace Too Fast

16 words in a rapid-fire recap may lose learners.

➜
SOLUTION

Rehearse the recap 5× for a clear, steady rhythm.

🏪

Filming the FamilyMart Scene

Stores don't always allow camera crews inside.

➜
SOLUTION

Film the kiosk quickly on-site, recreate the counter elsewhere.

📚

Too Many Vocabulary Words

Sixteen new words could overwhelm learners.

➜
SOLUTION

Teach in context with repeated exposure across scenes & exercises.
