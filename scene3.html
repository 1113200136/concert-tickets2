SCENE 3

🏪 Pickup at FamilyMart

One week later — Lily collects her physical ticket (with a sweet twist ending).

👤
Who
Lily, Maya, Zoe & Emma (now a clerk)
🕐
When & Where
One week later · FamilyMart
⏱
Length
~1 min 15 sec
🎯 Learning Aim: To know the vocabulary used at FamilyMart when picking up the ticket
🎬
Six Panels

Storyboard

MAYA: Okay. We're at the kiosk. Don't get nervous – this part is easy.
LILY: I see the menu. What do I press?
MAYA: SELECT "Ticket Pickup." Then enter your order number and press CONFIRM.
LILY: Entering the number… and confirm! (grabs the paper that prints out) LOOK! THE TICKETS!
MAYA: Lily. That's the payment slip. We give it to the clerk.
LILY: There's a two-step process?!
MAYA: Kiosk first, then clerk. And there's a 30-dollar printing fee.
LILY: We have to pay MORE?
MAYA: It's normal. Every store charges it. Let's go.
(They walk to Emma at the counter. Lily hands over the slip. Emma scans it – "Beep!")
EMMA (Clerk): 30-dollar printing fee, please. (hands over ticket and receipt) Here is your ticket and your RECEIPT. And please – do not post a photo of your ticket online. Someone can copy the barcode and enter the concert before you.
LILY: I will guard it with my life.
ZOE: So… if someone made a small mistake involving the refresh button… is there any chance there's a spare ticket?
LILY: (smiles slowly) Quantity: TWO, Zoe. One for me, one for you.
ZOE: You didn't.
LILY: I did. I bought yours before you even sat down.
MAYA: (already walking away) We all knew you were going to refresh.
LILY: You do owe me the 30-dollar printing fee, though.
ZOE: I will pay you ANYTHING.
