SCENE 2

⏰ On-Sale Battle (12:00 Noon)

The countdown hits zero — Lily races the website before the tickets vanish!

👤
Who
Lily, Maya, Zoe & Emma (the SYSTEM)
🕐
When & Where
12:00 noon · at home
⏱
Length
~2 minutes
🎯 Learning Aim: To understand the vocabulary and click-by-click flow of buying a ticket online
🎬
Six Panels

Storyboard

LILY: Okay. I logged in five minutes ago. Cable plugged into the router. Phone next to me. ID card right here. I am ready.
MAYA: Only one window open?
LILY: Only one. Two windows and the website thinks I'm a bot.
MAYA: Phone silenced? Credit card saved?
LILY: Both. Yes.
MAYA: Good. You might survive today. Ten… five… four… three… two… one – CLICK!
LILY: It's in! SESSION – Saturday night, December 14th!
MAYA: Next!
LILY: ZONE – standing area, near the stage!
MAYA: Next!
LILY: QUANTITY – two tickets!
EMMA (System): Please enter the VERIFICATION CODE to prove you are human.
LILY: Is that a capital W or lowercase w?!
MAYA: Just type it! SUBMIT! NOW!
EMMA (System): Processing. Please do not REFRESH the page.
ZOE: Mine is spinning – I'll just REFRESH it–
MAYA: ZOE, NO–
ZOE: Done. See? Now it'll–
EMMA (System): (to Zoe) Page refresh detected. You are now number 100,247 in line.
ZOE: …I refreshed it.
MAYA: You refreshed it.
EMMA (System): (to Lily) Congratulations! Your ORDER is locked. Please select your PAYMENT METHOD – credit card, ATM, or convenience store?
LILY: Credit card!
MAYA: Don't type it – PASTE it! Every second counts!
EMMA (System): Payment confirmed. Pick up your tickets at any FamilyMart after one week.
LILY: WE GOT THEM!
MAYA: Because you prepared correctly. As I said.
ZOE: (head on desk) Very happy for you both.
